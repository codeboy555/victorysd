Location
De Portola Middle School 11010 Clairemont Mesa Blvd, San Diego, CA 92124
Parking
Our facility has enough parking space for everyone. Simply follow the Victory signs and you will soon be greeted by our parking attendants who will guide you to the parking spaces reserved for first time guests.
Worship Service Experience
Expect a warm, welcoming, friendly atmosphere as you enter the hall. Snacks and drinks are available before and after the service. Experience inspiring worship, dynamic, practical and life-changing message, and encouraging fellowship every week. Service starts promptly at 10:30AM and ends at 12NN.
Attire
Dress like a normal SoCal person would…jeans, shorts, shirt, etc… But don’t forget, you are going to church not the beach!
Kids Church
Your children will love Victory Kids! With our trained volunteers and exciting kids’ environment, we’re sure your kids will feel the love of God, learn important truths, and have a great time. Kids Church experience is available for ages 2 to 7. You can check in your kids 15 minutes before the service. To find out more about Kids Church, click here.
